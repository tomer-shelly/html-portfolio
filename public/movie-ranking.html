The Best Movies According to Tomer

This next list contains my top 3 picks.

After

This is the first movie out of a series of 5 movies.
It's a romantic movie telling the story about how 2 teenagers met at random,
and started to fall in love despite the fact that one of them is in a commited relationship.
The following movies continue the story as thier life complicates.

Deadpool

This is a movie about a person that got experimented on and became immortal yet hideously scarred,
he sets out to track down the man who did this to him.
The best part about the movie is how funny it is and that the character is portrayed by one of my favorite actors, Ryan Reynolds.

Prisoners

The movie follows a dad that takes law into his own hands and the lengths he'd go to in order to bring home his missing daughter and her friend that disappeared.
